Title: Time in Reflection 1
Creator: Yili Wen
Introduction: This photographic work captures a nostalgic and peaceful scene. Through the reflector of the battery car, we can see the back of an old man in the old town. The atmosphere of life in the old city is very heavy, as if time has frozen here. The figure of the old man appeared so small and firm, presenting a kind of peace and beauty.
